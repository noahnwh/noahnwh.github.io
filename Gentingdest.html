Genting: The City of Entertainment in the Clouds

Experience the Thrills and Chills of Genting Highlands

Nestled in the lush Titiwangsa Mountains, Genting Highland offers an enchanting escape from the bustling city below, standing majestically 6,000 feet above sea level. Known for its breathtaking vistas, cool climate, and a plethora of exciting attractions, this renowned hill station provides a refreshing break from the everyday. The journey up the winding roads reveals a stunning panorama of verdant landscapes and misty clouds, creating an otherworldly atmosphere. At the heart of Genting Highland is Resorts World Genting, a sprawling entertainment complex featuring the exhilarating Genting SkyWorlds Theme Park, where thrilling rides and immersive experiences await. Visitors can also enjoy diverse dining options, from gourmet restaurants to casual eateries, and a range of luxurious accommodations offering stunning mountain views. For those seeking relaxation, the spa treatments and beautifully landscaped gardens provide a tranquil retreat. The cultural richness of the region is showcased through vibrant heritage shows, while nature enthusiasts can explore scenic highland trails. Genting Highland promises an unforgettable experience with its blend of excitement, relaxation, and natural beauty, making it a must-visit destination in Malaysia.




5 things that must do in traveling in Genting Highland!

1.Experience Genting SkyWorlds Theme Park: Immerse yourself in the thrills and excitement of Genting SkyWorlds Theme Park, the latest addition to Malaysia’s entertainment scene. This expansive park features a variety of themed zones, each offering unique attractions, from high-speed roller coasters to immersive interactive experiences. With state-of-the-art rides and attractions designed to entertain visitors of all ages, including family-friendly rides and heart-pounding adventures, Genting SkyWorlds promises an exhilarating day out. Enjoy the stunning views of the highland landscape as you explore the park, making it a perfect destination for both thrill-seekers and families.



2.Explore Resorts World Genting: Dive into the vibrant world of Resorts World Genting, a multifaceted entertainment complex that caters to every need and interest. This sprawling resort features luxurious hotels, a diverse range of dining options, and a variety of entertainment venues. Whether you're trying your luck at the casino, indulging in gourmet meals at one of the many restaurants, or catching a live show, there's something for everyone. The resort's dynamic atmosphere, combined with its high-end amenities and exceptional service, makes it a central hub for visitors seeking both relaxation and excitement.



3.Visit the SkyAvenue Mall: Discover SkyAvenue Mall, a modern shopping and entertainment destination that offers a blend of retail therapy, fine dining, and unique attractions. The mall boasts a wide array of international and local brands, catering to all shopping needs from luxury goods to everyday essentials. Savor culinary delights at its diverse range of restaurants and cafes, or enjoy a fun-filled day exploring attractions like the Cloud 9 experience, which provides stunning panoramic views and interactive displays. SkyAvenue Mall’s contemporary design and lively atmosphere make it a must-visit for those looking to enjoy shopping and leisure activities in a stylish setting.



4.Relax at the Awana Skyway:Take a breathtaking ride on the Awana Skyway cable car, which offers a serene and scenic journey through the highland’s lush landscapes. As you ascend, marvel at the panoramic views of the surrounding rainforest and distant cityscapes. The cable car ride provides a unique perspective of the region’s natural beauty, with clear views of the mountainous terrain and rolling clouds. It’s a tranquil experience that allows you to appreciate the area’s stunning vistas from a comfortable and leisurely vantage point, making it an ideal way to start or end your day in Genting Highland.



5.Discover Chin Swee Caves Temple: Explore the Chin Swee Caves Temple, a majestic and serene spiritual retreat nestled in the highlands. This impressive temple complex is renowned for its stunning architecture, intricate carvings, and panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. Wander through the beautifully landscaped gardens, marvel at the towering pagodas, and admire the various statues and sculptures that adorn the temple grounds. The temple offers a peaceful atmosphere for reflection and meditation, making it a perfect spot to escape the hustle and bustle of daily life. Its cultural significance and tranquil setting provide a rich and rewarding experience for visitors seeking both spiritual enrichment and breathtaking scenery.

Travel Description:

Day 1:
09:00 - Arrive in Genting Highland and check into your hotel. Start your day with a visit to the renowned Genting SkyWorlds Theme Park. Spend the morning exploring its themed zones, enjoying a variety of thrilling rides, and experiencing interactive attractions. Whether you're a fan of roller coasters or prefer more family-friendly options, there’s something for everyone.
13:00 - Enjoy a delicious lunch at one of the many dining options within the theme park or at the nearby SkyAvenue Mall. The mall offers a range of eateries from casual dining to fine cuisine.
15:00 - After lunch, explore SkyAvenue Mall, which boasts a vibrant mix of international retail stores, entertainment options, and unique attractions. Don’t miss the Cloud 9 experience for stunning panoramic views.
18:00 - As evening approaches, head to the Awana Skyway for a scenic cable car ride. Enjoy the serene views of the highland’s lush landscapes and the panoramic vistas as you travel from the base to the top of the mountain.
20:00 - Conclude your day with dinner at one of the fine restaurants in the Resorts World Genting complex. The variety of dining options ensures you can find something to suit your taste.

Day 2:
08:00 - Start your day with a visit to the Chin Swee Caves Temple, a peaceful spiritual retreat offering breathtaking views and serene gardens. Spend the morning exploring the temple's beautiful architecture and intricate carvings.
11:00 - Head back to the Resorts World Genting area and enjoy a leisurely lunch at a nearby restaurant or café.
13:00 - Dedicate the afternoon to exploring the various attractions within the Genting Highlands. Consider visiting the Genting Highlands Premium Outlets for some shopping or enjoying the array of entertainment options available at the resort.
17:00 - Spend the evening at the Genting Casino, one of the largest in Malaysia. Try your luck at the gaming tables or slot machines, or simply enjoy the vibrant atmosphere.
20:00 - For dinner, explore the diverse culinary options available within the Resorts World Genting complex, where you can enjoy a meal at a fine dining restaurant or a more casual eatery.

Day 3:
09:00 - After breakfast, take a leisurely walk around the Resorts World Genting area or enjoy a relaxing morning at the hotel. If time permits, visit the nearby attractions you might have missed, such as the First World Plaza.
12:00 - Have lunch at a restaurant of your choice within the Genting Highlands.
14:00 - Before you head back, visit any last spots you wish to see or simply unwind at a café, taking in the cool highland breeze and scenic views.
16:00 - Check out of your hotel and prepare for your departure, reflecting on your memorable 3-day adventure in Genting Highland.
